Brief CV

John Eric Tobias Lander

BA summa cum laude in Linguistics, Harvard University

Advanced MA summa cum laude in Linguistics, KU Leuven

PhD met felicitaties van de jury, Linguistics, Ghent University

I have held postdoctoral positions at the University of Gothenburg (GU) and Stockholm University (SU). I have taught linguistics at UGent, GU, SU, and Södertörns högskola. Currently I am part-time Senior Lecturer in linguistics at GU in the Department of Philosophy, Linguistics and Theory of Science.
