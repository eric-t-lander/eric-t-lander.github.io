I have worked on various topics:

articles, deixis, demonstratives, complementizers,

factivity, grammaticalization, ontological categories, and negation.

A number of my papers, often in collaboration with colleagues, have

approached crosslinguistic variation from a nanosyntactic perspective.

I am currently working on a large-scale study of sentential negation

in the early Nordic dialects, using runic, Eddic, and skaldic data.
