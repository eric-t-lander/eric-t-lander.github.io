Research

Eric T. Lander

I have worked on various topics: articles, deixis, demonstratives, complementizers, factivity, grammaticalization, ontological categories, and negation. A number of my papers, often in collaboration with colleagues, have approached crosslinguistic variation from a nanosyntactic perspective. I am currently working on a large-scale study of sentential negation in the early Nordic dialects, using runic, Eddic, and skaldic data. In addition to my pursuits in Old Germanic, I am also interested in linguistic theory and typology more generally.

Below is a list of my peer-reviewed publications, some of which can be downloaded. I also use this list as an opportunity to acknowledge and correct - to the best of my knowledge - any mistakes or errors on my part, so some of the papers include corrigenda.
